
Washing doesn’t only cleanse the item, but often fades color and removes structure. Items worn carefully and lightly can often be made fresh again by simply airing them out.

When it is time to do laundry, carefully read the tags on your clothes and divide accordingly. Not only lights with lights and darks with darks, but cool dry with cool dry and gentle rinse cycle with gentle rinse cycle items. 

Put delicates inside it before washing to prevent them from catching on the washer and other garments.

Fold clothes along the seems, to avoid unwanted creases and to maintain the shape of the item.
